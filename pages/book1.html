In this YA urban fantasy romance novel, a clueless and former rich girl works at a restaurant where she meets a family of witches who protect the world from evil spirits.

After her business tycoon father lost his fortune, Mia must navigate her new life without material wealth. She moves to a rundown studio apartment and transfers to a public school, where everyone hates her. When she needs money to attend a yacht party with her friends, she gets a part-time job at a takeout restaurant, where she keeps making mistakes, knocking things over and setting food on fire. One day at work, she finds her ex-boyfriend tied up in the basement.

Jason comes from a family of witches who run a Chinese restaurant. He and his sisters serve customers while secretly protecting the world from evil spirits.
