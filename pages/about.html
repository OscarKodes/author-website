About

Oscar Ko is an independent author who writes romantic stories with Chinese-American male leads, which may include fantasy or sci-fi elements. In New York, he took creative writing courses at CUNY and went to gradschool for a masters in DataVis. When Oscar is not writing, he can be found running, drinking tea, playing Wordle, watching anime, and puzzling about how to be more sociable.
